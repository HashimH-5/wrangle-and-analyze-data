WeRateDogs Analysis¶

Project Overview¶

WeRateDogs is a twitter account that started in 2015. The account rates people's dogs with humorous comment about the dog. WeRateDogs has over 4 million followers and has received international coverage

Intrduction¶

This analysis is of tweets from the WeRateDogs twitter account. My analysis revolved around not only the most loved dog stages but also the most rated and most retweeted dog stages and dog names

Analysis¶

In this analysis, a trend is recognised in the number of favorites and the number of retweets. In the figure below we see a positive correlation between the the number of retweets and the number of favorites. This shows that there is a high engagement of the WeRateDogs twitter account which reflects followers interests and preferences with dogs
